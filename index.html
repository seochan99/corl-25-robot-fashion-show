Open Call for Participation

Join the Future of Robotic Fashion

The Robot Fashion Show 2025 invites artists, designers, and researchers to propose creative ideas for "dressing up" robots for safety, aesthetic comfort, and creative expression.

This is your opportunity to be part of the first-ever cross-species haute couture experience, showcasing innovative approaches to robotic fashion and wearable technology.
